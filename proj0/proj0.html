Project 0

'Becoming Friends with Your Camera'
Part 1: Selfie

Part 1 Reflection

I took two images of myself at different distances. Some observations to note; the farther image my face looks flatter and 2D whereas the closer image mimics a 'fish-eye' effect, which makes sense and the closer the lens is to the image the more sensitive it is to the 3D structure.

Part 2: Architectural Perspective

Part 2 Insights

In the architectural perspective, I notice the same artifact as the selfie, where the first image was taken far away and the second image was taken closer up. The projection of the further image is wider, whereas the closer image looks narrower, which corroborates with the pattern learned in class.

Part 3: Dolly Zoom

Part 3 Discovery

I did a dolly zoom of my McDonalds Minecraft hamburger, as it was nice and geometric to observe any effect. Maybe it's because I did it with a cube, but the fish eye effect is more difficult to realize than with maybe like a sphere or something.
